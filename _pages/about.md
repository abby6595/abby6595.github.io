I am a Research Scientist and work at the intersection of wireless network, cybersecurity, and machine learning. In my research, I study cyber-physical systems, with a focus on how wireless components can efficiently against cyber threats for secure data sharing, authentication, and protection against tampering. I gound my work in experiments with medical IoT systems like drip infusion monitoring sensors and in user studies with hospitals participants.

I am pursuing my M.Sc. in Software and System Security at Oxford University. Previously, I obtained a M.S. in Computer Science and Information Engineering at NTUST and a Bachloar degree in Computer Science and Information Management at SCU.
